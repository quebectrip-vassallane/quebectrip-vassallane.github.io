The Vassal Lane French students are taking a trip to Quebec City Saturday from April 13 to Tuesday April 16, 2019 which is only days from now.

It is an expensive trip and we are planning some fundraising to assist a few families that otherwise wouldn't be able to go and bring down the cost for everyone. We set a goal of raising $6000 to support lowering the cost of the trip. Here is a summary of the fundraising events we are organizing to help lower the costs of the French Trip to Quebec. I hope everyone can find a way to be part of an event in the upcoming months. There are a few families who have requested specific levels of financial aid but we recognized that the trip is expensive, so we hope to bring down the costs for everyone.

How can I help?
